**Design Movement:** Japanese design restraint (Muji, Naoto Fukasawa) meets engineering documentation.

**Signature Elements:** Paper-texture noise overlays, copper accent lines referencing title blocks, depth shadows from consistent light source.

**Typography:** Fraunces (display) + Inter Tight (body) + IBM Plex Mono (metrics).
